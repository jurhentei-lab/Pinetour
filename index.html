Your Itinerary

1
Day
Ulaanbaatar
Sukhbaatar Square
Arrive in Mongolia's vibrant capital. Explore the historic central square, the National Museum, and Gandan Monastery. Evening welcome dinner with local cuisine.
City Tour Museum Welcome Dinner
2
Day
Bulgan Aimag
Amarbayasgalant Monastery
Drive north through rolling steppe to one of Mongolia's most beautiful Buddhist monasteries, nestled in a serene valley. Witness the harmony of Tibetan, Chinese and Mongolian architecture.
Heritage Buddhist Art Ger Camp
3
Day
Arkhangai Aimag
Khorgiin Togoo Volcano
Explore the remarkable extinct volcanic craters of Khorgiin Togoo. Trek around the twin craters and witness Mongolia's dramatic geological history up close.
Hiking Volcano Photography
4
Day
Arkhangai Aimag
Terkhin Tsagaan Lake
Discover the "White Lake," a stunning freshwater lake formed by ancient lava flows. Kayaking, birdwatching, and horseback riding amid breathtaking scenery.
Lake Kayaking Horse Riding
5
Day
Khuvsgul Aimag
Khuvsgul Lake
Journey to the "Dark Blue Pearl of Mongolia," one of the world's 17 ancient lakes. Crystal-clear waters surrounded by taiga forest and home to the nomadic Tsaatan reindeer herders.
Scenic Tsaatan Culture Boat Trip
